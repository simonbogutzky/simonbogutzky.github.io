2 Basic information

In this privacy policy, I inform you about the nature, scope and purpose of the collection and processing of your personal data. As the person responsible, I take data protection seriously and treat personal data confidentially and in accordance with the legal provisions.

3 Access data

3.1 Description, scope and purpose of data processing

When my websites (bogutzky.com, simonbogutzky.de, simonbogutzky.com) are accessed, the cloud provider or the cloud computing infrastructure automatically collects and stores data. This data includes the website visited, the date, time, size of the data sent, the browser used and the IP address used for access. When using the data, I do not draw any conclusions about you as a subject. The data is stored exclusively for security reasons, for example, to localize errors in the cloud computing infrastructure or to clarify cases of abuse.

The temporary storage of the IP address is necessary to enable delivery of the web pages to your end device. For this purpose, your IP address must remain stored for the duration of your session.

As provider I use GitHub and its infrastructure (GitHub Privacy Statement).

3.2 Legal basis for data processing

The legal basis for the temporary storage of data is Art. 6 ABS. 1 lit. f) DSGVO.

3.3 Duration of storage

GitHub automatically deletes the data as soon as the necessity for achieving the purpose of their collection expires. For the provision of my websites, this is the case when the respective session has ended.

4 Contact details

4.1 Description, scope and purpose of data processing

If you contact me using the contact options offered, I will store your information so that I can use it to process and respond to your request.

Without your consent, I will not pass on your personal data to third parties.

4.2 Legal basis for data processing

The legal basis for processing the data that you provide to me in the course of sending an e-mail is Art. 6 ABS. 1 lit. f) DSGVO.

The conversation is ended when it is clear from the circumstances that the matter in question has been conclusively clarified.

6 Your rights

You, as a user of my websites, have the right, in accordance with the DSGVO, to free information about which personal data I have stored about you. You also have the right to the correction of incorrect data and to a processing restriction or deletion of your personal data.

Unless your request conflicts with a legal obligation to retain data (e.g. data retention), you have the right to have your personal data deleted. I delete data stored by me when it is no longer necessary for its intended purpose and there are no legal retention periods. If deletion is not possible because the data is required for permissible legal purposes, data processing will be restricted. In this case, I will block the data and to prevent processing for other purposes.

If applicable, you can assert your right to data portability.

You can revoke your data protection consent at any time. If you wish a correction, blocking, deletion or information about the personal data stored about you or have questions regarding the collection, processing or use of your personal data, please contact: me@simonbogutzky.com

If you assume that I have processed your data unlawfully, you can file a complaint with the competent supervisory authority.
