2025

SnapTrack

Scan your Weight

SnapTrack was developed to save time during daily weight and body fat tracking. Using your iPhone's camera, the app scans digital scale displays and automatically saves your weight and body fat data into the Health app.

Settings allow you to adjust recognition parameters for digital displays and disable body fat scanning if needed.

GoodHabits

Build better habits in 66 days

A proof of concept exploring declarative programming with SwiftUI. GoodHabits guides you on a 66-day journey to better behaviors through customizable goals, daily tracking, home screen widgets, and smart reminders.

LIVE.STAND

Sit-stand desk companion

A Bluetooth-connected app that reminds you to alternate between sitting and standing throughout the workday. Built solo in Swift (iOS) and Kotlin (Android) using UIKit, CoreData, CoreBluetooth, SwiftUI, Combine, and RxAndroid.

IlcoControl

Bluetooth control for care beds

A white-label Bluetooth control app for care beds with customizable corporate design per client. Built in Swift using UIKit and CoreBluetooth. My first app written entirely in Swift.

FlowMeter

Measure subjective flow states

An app for tracking subjective "flow" experiences per Csíkszentmihályi's theory, paired with heart rate data from a Bluetooth HR chest strap. Written in Objective-C with UIKit, CoreData, and CoreBluetooth.
